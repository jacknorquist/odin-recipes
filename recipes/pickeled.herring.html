Pickeled Herring

Pickled Herring in a Jar

In this article we'll show you how to make your very own pickled herring. This will take around 30 min, not including brine time.

Ingredients

  • Herring/Tullibee/Ciscoe
  • All Spice
  • White Distilled Vinegar
  • Sugar
  • Some Other Stuff

Steps

  1. Fillet the fish. You can leave the bones in, the accidity discolves the bones
  2. Brine the fillets for 24 hours in saltwater. This make the fish firm
  3. Combine distilled vinegar, alspice, bay leaves, lemons, onions, and sugar into a jar.
  4. Add the herring fillets. Let sit in fridge for 24 hours.
  5. Enjoy!
